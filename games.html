Featured Games

The Velvet Room

Manage a bar in a foreign city. Mix drinks, upgrade your establishment, and meet customers.

Perhaps you'll soon realize that there's more to this city than meets the eye...

  • Bar Management - Run and manage a stylish bar in a foreign city.
  • Drink Mixing - Craft cocktails using a variety of ingredients to satisfy customers' tastes.
  • Customer Interaction - Engage with unique patrons, each with their own backstory and secrets.
  • Upgrades & Expansion - Improve your bar with better furniture, decor, and high-end liquor.
  • Mystery & Intrigue - Uncover hidden stories about the city and its people as you serve drinks.
  • Choice-Driven Dialogue - Your interactions with customers influence relationships and outcomes.

The Top Performer

As a server at the most renowned restaurant in town, you bring in more tips than you can count. But one day, you encounter a check for 3 million as your tip amount.

With this, you dive head first into the dark side of your business.

  • Decision-Making: Choose how to handle the money—report it, spend it, or investigate its origins.
  • Uncover Secrets: The tip leads you into the underground world of crime, corruption, and power.
  • Interactive Dialogue: Engage with shady characters, law enforcement, and wealthy elites.
  • Action and Strategy: Participate in high-stakes deals, and climb the ranks of the underworld.
  • Multiple Endings: Your choices determine whether you thrive, escape, or fall victim to your decisions.

Fortune's Curse

As a cashier at a struggling convenience store, you sell a winning lottery ticket worth $500 million. But when the ticket buyer mysteriously disappears, you’re left holding the jackpot.

With this, your family and friends start to turn on you, and trust becomes a luxury you can’t afford.

  • The Temptation: You find yourself in possession of a fortune with no rightful owner in sight.
  • Family & Friends Turn: As rumors spread, those closest to you start acting differently.
  • Social Manipulation: Navigate jealousy, betrayal, and shifting relationships.
  • Trust or Isolation: Decide who to share your wealth with and who to cut off.
  • Psychological Battle: Experience gaslighting, guilt trips, and emotional confrontations.
  • Legal & Ethical Risks: Face investigations, lawsuits, and moral dilemmas.
